About FreqShield

FreqShield is a novel audio protection system that safeguards your voice from malicious cloning. Instead of adding complex noise or perturbations, our system is based on a paradigm we call "Information Subtraction." It employs a fundamental signal processing technique—a high-pass filter—to strategically remove the low-frequency cues that voice cloning models depend on, while preserving the natural sound quality for human listeners.
